- **Boundary:** guidance in prompts versus deterministic enforcement and runtime policy
- **Invariant:** instructions are scoped, discoverable, non-conflicting, and subordinate to system authority
- **Default evidence:** frontmatter/schema validation, discovery tests, negative fixtures, and task outcomes
- **Risk classes:** ai, governance, context
