# Product System

## Product Thinking

A product is not a list of features. It is a promise to a user under constraints.

Every feature must answer:

- Who needs this?
- What job does it perform?
- Why now?
- What outcome changes?
- What can go wrong?
- How will we know it worked?
- What complexity does it introduce?

## Feature Lifecycle

1. Problem definition
2. User/context mapping
3. Success metric
4. Scope boundary
5. Design/architecture
6. Build
7. Validation
8. Documentation
9. Review
10. Follow-up risks
