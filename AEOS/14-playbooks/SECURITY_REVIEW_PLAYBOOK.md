# Security Review Playbook

## Steps

1. Identify protected assets.
2. Identify entry points.
3. Identify trust boundaries.
4. Identify attacker-controlled inputs.
5. Identify persistence effects.
6. Identify replay/duplicate paths.
7. Identify logging/backup leaks.
8. Verify controls.
9. Name residual risk.

## Output

Lead with findings. Then give recommended order of fixes.
