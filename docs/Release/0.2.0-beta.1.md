## Included

- Local event ledgers for Money in and Money out.
- Reviewed on-device ML Kit receipt OCR with confidence and duplicate gates.
- Explicit contributor/vendor attribution separated from OCR counterparty evidence.
- Persisted members, transactions, app-private receipt JSON, and explicit Room migrations.
- Permanent-impact acknowledgement before transaction deletion.
- Local-only disclosure, Trust Center, Privacy, Terms, support, and manual update checks.

## Important Limits

- This is a beta for testing, not the only record for important or irreplaceable money data.
- Each installation owns an independent local ledger. There is no account, authentication, authorization, or cross-device synchronization.
- Public/private is a local marker, not access control.
- Event-copy links do not verify people or synchronize balances, members, transactions, or receipts.
- OCR can be wrong and requires human review.
- There is no export, restore, cloud backup, or recovery after uninstall/device loss.
- The app records ledger evidence; it does not collect, hold, transfer, or invest money.
