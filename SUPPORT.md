## Private Support And Privacy

Email [banothgopikrishna19@gmail.com](mailto:banothgopikrishna19@gmail.com?subject=Community%20Ledger%20support) for privacy questions, sensitive app reports, or support that cannot be discussed publicly.

## Public Bug Reports

Use [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/GopiB9119/event/issues) for non-sensitive reproducible bugs and documentation problems. Search existing issues first.

Never post receipt images, member details, phone numbers, email addresses, UPI IDs, transaction references, passwords, signing keys, or verification codes in a public issue.

## Security Reports

Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Suspected vulnerabilities must be reported privately.

## Payment And Fundraising Disputes

Community Ledger does not collect, hold, transfer, recover, or invest money. It cannot reverse a payment, verify a fundraiser, or contact a bank/payment provider on a user's behalf. Contact the relevant organizer and payment provider directly.
